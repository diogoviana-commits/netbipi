# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want NetBIPI to be a welcoming open source project for operators,
developers, contributors and users of all backgrounds.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Give feedback on the work, not on the person.
- Assume good faith until there is evidence otherwise.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Share reproductions, logs and context when reporting problems.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination or personal attacks.
- Doxxing, threats or intimidation.
- Spam, trolling or disruptive conduct.
- Sharing secrets, private credentials or unrelated personal data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or restrict participation when
behavior harms the project or its community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, report it privately to the project
maintainer or through a private security or contact channel if one is available.
